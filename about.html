About us

Welcome to Leyendas, a unique themed bar that transports you to a world of ancestral stories and legends in every corner. The bar is decorated with elements that evoke the magical essence of these stories, creating an atmosphere that immerses you in the culture and tradition of Guatemala.

Our cocktail menu is a masterpiece of mixology, where each drink tells a story and each sip is a unique experience. From the "Manto de la Siguanaba" to the "Barco de la Tatuana", our cocktails are carefully crafted to capture the essence of these legends and awaken your senses.

Whether you want to explore the rich history of Guatemala through cocktails or simply enjoy a charming atmosphere with friends, Leyendas offers you an unforgettable experience.

Join us and let yourself be carried away by the magic of Guatemalan legends in this one-of-a-kind themed bar.
