Dive into the magic of Leyendas, where legends are sipped more than read. Here, stories come alive, and you become the protagonist of an endless adventure. Cheers to the legends of Guatemala and the magic that lives in every corner of this place!

* Cocktail presentation may vary.

Corazón del Cielo Azul

A liquid celebration of Guatemala's independence. This cocktail captivates with a vibrant blend of gin and blue curaçao, evoking the colors of our flag. The freshness of lemon adds a citrusy touch that awakens the senses, while a delicate foam crowns the drink, giving it a smooth texture and sophistication. Each sip is a tribute to the freedom and mysticism of our land, where legends blend with history. Perfect for toasting to the homeland with style and tradition.

El beso del demonio

A cocktail that immerses you in mystery and forbidden pleasure. Tequila and vodka intertwine in a fiery embrace, while lemon and passionfruit provide a citrusy and tropical explosion. Pineapple juice softens the spell, leaving a sweet and tempting trail. This golden, seductive elixir pays tribute to Guatemala's dark legends—a burning kiss that takes you to the edge of the unknown, where pleasure and danger meet.

Las lágrimas de la Llorona

Under the full moon, in the shadows of lost rivers, fall the sweet tears of La Llorona—each drop a blend of nostalgia and pleasure. A sweet and mysterious embrace of vodka and amaretto, wrapped in the freshness of mint chocolate, with citrus notes of lemon and orange that awaken the senses. Topped with a soft foam, like La Llorona's own tears, perfect for those seeking comfort in sweetness and legend.

El manto de la Siguanaba

Smoky mezcal meets the floral delicacy of St. Germain, enhanced with tropical pineapple, fresh lemon, and golden cardamom. A dark and mysterious cocktail that evokes the intriguing presence of the Siguanaba in every sip.

El barco de la Tatuana

A journey of exceptional flavors beginning with distinctive Jameson whiskey blended with Bianco vermouth. This cocktail is a voyage that sets sail toward the enchantment of La Tatuana.

El espíritu del Cadejo

This elixir combines the warmth of rum with the enigma of Campari, balanced by the freshness of pineapple juice and the vibrant touch of lemon juice. St. Germain adds a subtle floral fragrance, elevating each sip to a golden dawn. The intense orange color evokes the first rays of sun illuminating the mythical land of Xibalbá, inviting you to savor the awakening of a new legend.

El beso de la Siguanaba

An intriguing cocktail that fuses gin infused with blue tea flower and gin infused with bay leaf, enriched with blue tea infusion and the sweetness of Oleo Saccharum. A kiss that evokes the mysterious presence of the Siguanaba.

La ruta del Cadejo Negro

A fascinating cocktail combining smooth Monkey Shoulder with the sweetness of banana cream and the depth of Amaro Montenegro. Clarified peanut butter adds a unique texture and nutty nuance.

La ruta del Cadejo Blanco

This intriguing cocktail blends the elegance of Beefeater 24 gin with Torino vermouth and the complexity of Cynar. Lemon juice adds a fresh citrus touch, while Orleans bitters contribute depth and mystery.

El Sombrerón enamorado

An intriguing blend of vodka and Frangelico, enhanced by the citrus touch of lemon juice and the sweetness of violet cream. Hazelnut falernum and neutral syrup add complexity and balance, creating a captivating flavor experience.

La penitencia de la Llorona

A bold fusion of young mezcal and Cucha, with the citrusy touch of lemon and the mystery of falernum #2. Clarified apple juice and syrup soften and balance this flavor experience, creating a tempting penance in every sip.

El Penitente Clásico

A smoky blend of whiskey, Amaretto, Cocchi Bianco, and hazelnut syrup. A cocktail of intriguing character that evokes penance with its deep smoky flavor, sweet-and-sour notes, and a light spicy touch.

Entre Volcanes

Between fiery volcanoes, where fire rises, hides an intoxicating aroma of whiskey, lemon, and lava. The golden aged liquor blends with citrus juice, creating a dance of flavors that leaves its mystique on the lips.
