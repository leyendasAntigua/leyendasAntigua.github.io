Dive into the magic of Leyendas, where legends are sipped more than read. Here, stories come alive, and you become the protagonist of an endless adventure. Cheers to the legends of Guatemala and the magic that lives in every corner of this place!

* Cocktail presentation may vary.

Corazón del Cielo Azul

A liquid celebration of Guatemala's independence. This cocktail captivates with a vibrant blend of gin and blue curaçao, evoking the color of our flag. The freshness of lemon adds a citrusy touch, while a delicate foam crowns the drink with softness and sophistication. Each sip pays tribute to freedom and the mysticism of our land, where legends blend with history.

El beso del demonio

A cocktail that immerses you in mystery and forbidden pleasure. Tequila and vodka intertwine in a fiery embrace, while lemon and passion fruit deliver a tropical explosion. Pineapple juice softens the spell, leaving a sweet and tempting trace. A golden and seductive elixir inspired by Guatemala's dark legends.

Lágrimas de la Llorona

Under the full moon, in the shadows of lost rivers, fall the sweet tears of La Llorona, each drop a blend of nostalgia and delight. A mysterious embrace of vodka and amaretto, wrapped in cool mint chocolate, with lemon and orange citrus to awaken the senses. A soft foam is added, like the very tears of La Llorona, perfect for those who seek comfort in sweetness and legend.

El manto de la Siguanaba

Smoky mezcal meets the floral delicacy of Saint Germain, highlighted by tropical pineapple, fresh lemon, and golden cardamom. A dark and mysterious cocktail that evokes the enigmatic presence of the Siguanaba in every sip.

El barco de la Tatuana

A journey of exceptional flavors begins with distinctive Jameson whiskey mixed with Bianco vermouth. This cocktail sets sail toward the charm of La Tatuana.

El espíritu del Cadejo

This elixir blends the warmth of rum with the mystery of Campari, balanced by fresh pineapple juice and the vibrant touch of lemon juice. Saint Germain adds a subtle floral fragrance, elevating each sip to a golden dawn. The intense orange hue recalls the first rays of sun that light up mythical Xibalbá.

El beso de la Siguanaba

An intriguing cocktail blending gin infused with blue tea flower and gin infused with laurel, enriched with blue tea infusion and the sweetness of Oleo Saccharum. A kiss that evokes the mysterious presence of the Siguanaba.

La ruta del Cadejo Negro

A fascinating cocktail combining smooth Monkey Shoulder whiskey with banana cream and the depth of Amaro Montenegro. Clarified peanut butter gives it a unique texture and a nutty touch.

La ruta del Cadejo Blanco

This intriguing cocktail mixes elegant Beefeater 24 gin with Vermouth di Torino and the complexity of Cynar. Lemon juice adds a fresh citrusy touch, while Bitter Orleans brings depth and mystery.

El Sombrerón enamorado

An intriguing blend of vodka and Frangelico, enhanced by lemon juice and the sweetness of violet cream. Hazelnut falernum and neutral syrup add complexity and balance for a captivating flavor experience.

La penitencia de la Llorona

A bold fusion of young mezcal and Cucha, with a citrusy lemon touch and the mystery of Falernum #2. Syrup and clarified apple juice balance and soften this flavorful experience, creating a tempting penance in every sip.

El Penitente Clásico

A smoky mix of whiskey, amaretto, Cocchi Bianco, and hazelnut syrup. An intriguing cocktail that evokes penance with its deep smoky flavor, bittersweet notes, and subtle spice.

Entre Volcanes

Among burning volcanoes, where fire rises, a heady aroma hides—whiskey, lemon, and lava. The golden, aged liquor mixes with citrus juice, creating a dance of flavors that leaves mysticism on your lips.
